Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order custom photo magnets?
Simply upload your photos through our website, select your quantity (square or round format), and we'll take care of the rest. Each magnet is handmade with care and shipped directly to you.
What shapes and sizes are available?
We offer 2x2 inch square photo magnets and Save the Date magnets in 3.25x4 inch or 2.75x3.5 inch sizes. Square format is our specialty!
How long does shipping take?
We ship within the United States via USPS and UPS. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days after your order is processed.
Can I order in bulk for events?
Yes! Our Save the Date magnets come in sets up to 100. Contact us for special bulk pricing on weddings and events.
