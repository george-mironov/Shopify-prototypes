Our Story

Hi, I'm Ivette

As a passionate photo taker and magnet collector, I created Magnet Love to combine my two loves. From disposable to digital cameras, I've always cherished capturing memories.

With thousands of photos stored digitally, I realized what better way of displaying them than on magnets! This inspired me to craft beautiful, handmade photo magnets—sharing my passion as I help others showcase their precious memories.

Everything you need to know about our handmade photo magnets.

Our Custom Photo Magnets are 2×2 inches square. Save The Date Magnets come in two sizes: 3.25×4 inches and 2.75×3.5 inches rectangular.

We ship via USPS and UPS within the United States only. Standard processing takes 3-5 business days, plus shipping time depending on your location.

Absolutely! Our Save The Date magnets come in sets from 10 to 100+. Custom Photo Magnets come in sets of 4 or 6. Contact us for larger custom orders.

We accept JPEG, PNG, and HEIC formats. For best results, please use high-resolution images (at least 1000×1000 pixels for square magnets).

Our Save The Date vinyl magnets are water-resistant. All magnets are made with high-quality materials designed to last for years.

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We hope to expand internationally in the future!
